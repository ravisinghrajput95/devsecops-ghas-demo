## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, **please do not open a public issue**.

Instead, report it securely through one of the following channels:

- **Email:** security@yourorg.com  
- **GitHub Security Advisory:** [Report a vulnerability](../../security/advisories/new)

We aim to:
- Respond to all vulnerability reports **within 48 hours**  
- Provide a remediation or mitigation plan **within 5 business days** (depending on severity)

---

## Disclosure Policy
Once the vulnerability is confirmed and fixed, we will:
1. Acknowledge the reporter (if they wish).  
2. Publish an advisory summarizing the issue, impact, and fix.  
3. Include references to the patched release or commit.
